# BrainVault – Product Understanding & Development Plan (Version 1.0)

---

# Vision

BrainVault is an **Agentic AI-powered Personal Knowledge Brain** that automatically captures, understands, organizes, and retrieves knowledge from multiple sources.

Unlike traditional note-taking applications, BrainVault does not simply store information. It intelligently identifies the type of content, extracts valuable information, classifies it into the appropriate knowledge space, organizes it according to difficulty and topic, and builds a continuously evolving personal knowledge ecosystem.

BrainVault acts as a **Second Brain**, enabling users to save everything they learn and retrieve it in an organized, AI-curated manner whenever they want to study, revise, research, or create new content.

---

# Problem Statement

Professionals, AI engineers, researchers, and students consume enormous amounts of valuable information every day from sources such as:

* LinkedIn posts
* PDFs
* Research papers
* Medium blogs
* Documentation
* ChatGPT conversations
* AI-generated content
* Technical notes
* Interview questions
* Personal observations
* Tweets/X posts
* YouTube transcripts
* GitHub repositories

Unfortunately, this knowledge is scattered across multiple platforms.

Users typically bookmark posts, save PDFs to random folders, maintain disconnected notes, or forget where they originally found useful information.

Even when the information is saved, it remains difficult to:

* Find later
* Organize by topic
* Understand relationships
* Study progressively
* Retrieve instantly
* Reuse for projects
* Generate new content from existing knowledge

Current note-taking tools require manual organization and cannot reason about the stored knowledge.

There is currently no intelligent personal system capable of automatically understanding incoming information, determining its context, categorizing it appropriately, organizing it for future learning, and allowing natural interaction with the accumulated knowledge.

BrainVault solves this problem through an **Agentic AI architecture**.

# Frontend Vision

This should **not** resemble a basic CRUD dashboard. It should feel like a polished SaaS product (similar in quality to Notion AI, Perplexity, or ChatGPT), with a strong visual identity around the concept of a digital brain.

## Landing/Home Page

The homepage serves as both a marketing landing page and the primary interaction surface.

Sections include:

* **Hero section** with animated BrainVault branding (neural network/brain visualization), headline, and short description.
* **Large universal input box** where users can paste any content (URLs, text, notes, prompts, etc.).
* **Smart detection status** showing how the AI is classifying and processing the input in real time.
* **Recently added knowledge** cards.
* **Quick navigation** to major knowledge spaces.
* **Search bar** for semantic search.
* **Statistics** (documents stored, categories, reading time, knowledge graph size).
* **Suggested learning paths** generated from stored knowledge.

## Knowledge Pages

Each knowledge space (LinkedIn, Blogs, Research Papers, Interview Questions, AI Notes, etc.) has:

* Interactive cards with thumbnails and metadata.
* Filters by topic, difficulty, source, and tags.
* Timeline and hierarchical views.
* Full-screen reading mode for PDFs and long-form content.
* AI summary and "Ask AI" panel.
* Related content recommendations.

## Navigation

A persistent sidebar or floating navigation provides access to:

* Dashboard
* LinkedIn Knowledge
* Blogs
* Research Papers
* Interview Questions
* AI Notes
* Prompt Library
* Documentation
* GitHub Repositories
* Learning Paths
* AI Chat
* Settings
